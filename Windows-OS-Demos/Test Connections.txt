Ping same On Ubuntu & Windows:
ping google.ca

Trace-Route On Windows:
tracert google.ca

On Ubuntu:
traceroute google.ca

Testing Net Connection:

On Windows:
Test-NetConnection google.ca

On Ubuntu:
nc -z -v google.ca 443
Connection to google.ca (142.251.41.35) 443 port [tcp/https] succeeded!

nc -z -v google.ca 80
Connection to google.ca (142.251.41.35) 80 port [tcp/http] succeeded!

Client/Server Model Ubuntu Demo:
On Terminal 1 : Start listening on Port 1234
nc -l 1234

On Terminal 2 : Connect to LocalHost at 1234 
nc -N 127.0.0.1 1234

To simulate a Chat App, we introduce a 10 second delay:
nc -i 10 127.0.0.1 1234

NSLookup same On Ubuntu & Windows:
nslookup google.ca
