NTP Demo: The minute we change the TimeZone Date is Synchronized in all the Places

On Ubuntu Terminal and Windows PowerShell:

On Ubuntu:
On Terminal : 
$ date
Thu Mar 13 03:42:11 PM EDT 2025

On Browser Developer Console :
new Date()
Thu Mar 13 2025 15:43:19 GMT-0400 (Eastern Daylight Time)
﻿
Ubuntu:
Settings -> System -> Date and Time -> Time Zone -> Change to Melbourne Australia

On Terminal : 
$ date
Fri Mar 14 06:44:56 AM AEDT 2025

On Browser Developer Console :
new Date()
Fri Mar 14 2025 06:45:43 GMT+1100 (Australian Eastern Daylight Time)

Windows :
On PowerShell (Close after showing for EDT and Re-Open for the Next TimeZone):
Get-Date
Thursday, March 13, 2025 3:56:25 PM 

On Browser Developer Console :
new Date()
Thu Mar 13 2025 15:43:19 GMT-0400 (Eastern Daylight Time)
﻿
Windows:
Date and Time Settings from Start OR
Settings -> Time and Language -> -> Time Zone -> Change to Seoul Korea

On PowerShell:
Get-Date
Friday, March 14, 2025 5:02:04 AM

On Browser Developer Console :
new Date()
Date Fri Mar 14 2025 05:00:28 GMT+0900 (Korean Standard Time)
